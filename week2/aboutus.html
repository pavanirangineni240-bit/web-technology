About VCE:

Vardhaman College of Engineering (VCE) is an autonomous institution in Shamshabad, Hyderabad, established in 1999 and affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad. It holds NAAC A++ accreditation and NBA Tier-I accreditation, recognized for its academic excellence and student success. The college offers various engineering programs, including Computer Science, Information Technology, Electronics, and Mechanical Engineering. It emphasizes practical-oriented education, industry tie-ups, and innovative teaching methods to prepare students for the professional world. Location The campus is located in Kacharam, Shamshabad, near the Bangalore Highway (NH-7). It is approximately a 30-minute drive from the Hyderabad International Airport and close to the outer ring road. NAAC with an A++ Grade and NBA (Tier-I, Washington Accord), the institution offers a comprehensive range of academic programs including B.Tech, M.Tech, and MBA, catering to a large and diverse student community. With UGC autonomous status valid up to 2030–31 and recognition under Sections 2(f) and 12(B), VCE has consistently demonstrated academic excellence, quality assurance, and institutional credibility. The institution is committed to delivering outcome-based education supported by modern pedagogical practices such as experiential learning, problem-based learning, and participative learning. VCE boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, advanced laboratories, centers of excellence, and a well-equipped digital library. Strong industry collaborations, active MoUs, and a dedicated Career Development Cell (CDC) ensure that students are industry-ready, reflected in consistently high placement outcomes. The institution also promotes innovation and entrepreneurship through its Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (CIE), fostering a culture of creativity and start-up development. Vardhaman has established itself as a hub for research and innovation, with significant funding support, extensive publications, and a strong patent portfolio. Faculty and students actively engage in national and international research collaborations with organizations such as ISRO, DRDO, and IISc. The institution is also deeply committed to sustainability, social responsibility, and holistic student development, aligning its initiatives with NEP 2020 and global best practices. Through continuous improvement, global engagement, and community-oriented initiatives, VCE continues to strengthen its position as a leading institution in engineering education and research.
